# Quotes

The `quote` callout drops the usual left bar for three editorial treatments.

## Illuminated

Edge-to-edge accent wash with a large drop-quote mark.

> [!quote|illum] Leonardo da Vinci
> Learning never exhausts the mind.

## Gloss

Quiet marginal gloss — italic serif, minimal chrome.

> [!quote|gloss] Leonardo da Vinci
> Art is never finished, only abandoned.

## Tablet

Inscribed-tablet corner brackets.

> [!quote|tab] Leonardo da Vinci
> Obstacles cannot crush me; every obstacle yields to stern resolve.

## Syntax

```markdown
> [!quote|illum] Attribution
> The quotation text.
```

**Use it for:** `illum` → a hero pull-quote to anchor a section. `gloss` → understated supporting quotes. `tab` → epigraphs / inscriptions. The title becomes the attribution line.
