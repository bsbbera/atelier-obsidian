# Callouts

All 13 native Obsidian callout types, restyled into the excavation palette. No cast needed — just the type.

> [!note] Note
> A plain margin note. Copper title, hairline rule.

> [!info] Info
> Neutral information block.

> [!tip] Tip
> Helpful aside. (Aliases: `hint`, `important`.)

> [!success] Success
> Confirmed / done. (Aliases: `check`, `done`.)

> [!question] Question
> Open question. (Aliases: `help`, `faq`.)

> [!warning] Warning
> Proceed with care. (Aliases: `caution`, `attention`.)

> [!danger] Danger
> Critical. (Alias: `error`.)

> [!example] Example
> A worked example.

> [!quote] Quote
> A blockquote callout. (Alias: `cite`.)

> [!abstract] Abstract
> Summary / TL;DR. (Aliases: `summary`, `tldr`.)

> [!todo] Todo
> A task list lives well here.

> [!bug] Bug
> Known issue.

> [!failure] Failure
> Something that did not work. (Aliases: `fail`, `missing`.)

## Syntax

```markdown
> [!note] Optional title
> Body text. Drop the title to use the default label.

> [!warning]- Collapsed by default
> The trailing `-` makes it foldable.
```

**Use it for:** the everyday semantic blocks. For the dressed-up variants (stamps, flags, tablets), see [Emotion casts](11-emotion-casts.md).
