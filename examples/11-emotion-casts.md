# Emotion casts

Each semantic callout has dressed-up variants — stamps, flags, tablets, tags. Same `> [!type|cast]` pattern. Pick the mood that fits.

| Type | Casts |
|------|-------|
| `note` | `card` · `slip` · `pin` |
| `info` | `placard` · `plaque` · `entry` |
| `tip` | `flag` · `lamp` · `dogear` |
| `success` | `stamp` · `tick` · `ribbon` |
| `warning` | `tape` · `stencil` · `barrier` |
| `question` | `query` · `tag` · `ponder` |
| `danger` | `condemn` · `fragile` · `klaxon` |
| `example` | `specimen` · `plate` · `tray` |
| `todo` | `checklist` · `manifest` |

## A few rendered

> [!success|stamp] Attribution confirmed
> Authenticated as autograph after cleaning revealed the *pentimenti*.

> [!warning|barrier] A fragile experiment
> An oil-on-dry-plaster technique that began flaking within his lifetime.

> [!info|placard] On the mirror writing
> Probably not secrecy — he was left-handed and dragged the pen to avoid smudging.

> [!example|specimen] The heart
> Described as a four-chambered muscle, with valve mechanics only confirmed by MRI centuries later.

> [!tip|lamp] Tested, 500 years late
> A parachute built to his pyramid design worked exactly as predicted.

> [!danger|fragile] Lost to time
> The planned anatomy treatise was never published; the drawings vanished for 260 years.

## Syntax

```markdown
> [!success|stamp] Title
> Body.

> [!todo|checklist] This week
> - [ ] item one
> - [x] item two
```

**Use it for:** giving semantic blocks personality without leaving the callout system. Unknown cast → the plain callout renders. See also [Callouts](01-callouts.md).
